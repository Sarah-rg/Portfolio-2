Digital Marketing


Live Project - Angee Sweet Cakes

We had the opportunity to work on a live project in groups. I worked with Marija, Maisie and Irene to analyse and overhaul the digital presence of a local business, Angee Sweet Cakes. She had two brands, two Instagram and Facebook accounts, and was overwhelmed - she hadn't posted for two and a half months when we started working on her brand.


We prepared a Digital Marketing Strategy, identifying key customers, results we would like to see, and an outline of the content we would create and social media platforms that would be used.


Using the strategy that we had developed, we created a Content Marketing Calendar to plan what posts would be shared and when.



This included updating her facebook banner to match the new branding, with information to reflect her Jar Cakes branding and product offerings.



Content Creation

We designed posts for Angee that were consistent with the branding - using the same font and colour palette for posts. Identifying where she had gaps in her content, we used photos from her website to create content that would fill the gaps.


Live Project - Minnow.tech

Using this format, I developed a small digital marketing campaign concept for Minnow.tech. They are a business offering support to small and medium sized businesses, and wanted to attract prospective clients in the UK.


The landing page of Minnow.tech's website - they have a strong brand identity, colour scheme and nice design so I didn't want to mess with their branding.



I identified key market groups that Minnow.tech should be targeting, and considered information that they would like to know if they had never heard of the company before. These could be worked into a marketing strategy for key posts for particular days of the week.


Content Creation

Using their branding style, I created a range of content that would be appealing to prospective customers, showcasing the services Minnow.tech offer.
